Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about my development services, project timelines, and what to expect when working together.

1. What technologies do you specialize in for web development?

I specialize in modern frontend technologies including React.js, Next.js, TypeScript, and Tailwind CSS. For backend development, I work with Node.js, Express.js, MongoDB, and Firebase. I also have experience with WordPress development and can create custom themes and plugins.

2. How long does it typically take to complete a project?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity. A simple website typically takes 2-4 weeks, while complex web applications can take 8-12 weeks. Mobile apps usually require 6-10 weeks. I always provide detailed timelines during the initial consultation and keep you updated throughout the development process.

3. Do you provide ongoing maintenance and support after launch?

Yes, I offer ongoing maintenance packages that include regular updates, security patches, performance optimization, and technical support. This ensures your application remains secure, up-to-date, and performs optimally. I can also provide training for your team to manage content updates.

4. Can you work with existing systems or integrate with third-party services?

Absolutely! I have extensive experience integrating with existing systems, APIs, and third-party services like payment gateways (Stripe, PayPal), CRM systems, email marketing platforms, and social media APIs. I can also help migrate legacy systems to modern technologies while preserving your data.

5. What is your approach to responsive design and mobile optimization?

I follow a mobile-first approach, ensuring all websites and applications work seamlessly across all devices. I use modern CSS frameworks like Bootstrap and Tailwind CSS, implement progressive web app features, and optimize for Core Web Vitals to ensure excellent user experience and SEO performance on all screen sizes.
